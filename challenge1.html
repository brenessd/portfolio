Multivariate Data Visualization:

The purpose of this challenge is to practice designing visualizations of data. You have been provided a .txt file containing 17 different attributes for 86 Irish whiskies. The key to this assignment is to figure out: what information you want to represent, what to omit, and how to represent it.

Objective

Design a static visualization of this data set that will convey its key characteristics to the viewer.

Constraints:

  • You should represent your visualization on the front of a single piece of paper.
  • You don't need to faithfully and accurately map the entire data set but you should provide enough detail so that the idea of your visualization is clear.
  • You are not allowed to use software that assists in visualization design and layout.
