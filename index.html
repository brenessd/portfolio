An Introduction.

Hi. My name is Stacy and I'm a Mathematics Ph.D. student at Clarkson University in Potsdam, New York.

The collection of data visualizations below were all created during the Fall 2021 Information Visualization class given at Clarkson University. The scope of the work is vast and ranges in complexity. Here, you'll find static images with ideas for how to best capture the characteristics of Irish whiskies and an adjacency matrix transformed into artwork alongside dynamic dashboards in R built with Shiny.io.

Of all the work presented, my favorite is that which is closest to my heart — a dashboard for parents of children diagnosed with cancer made using data from the National Cancer Institute's Childhood Cancer Data Initiative. With it, parents can input their child's demographic and diagnostic information to obtain the incidence rates and the survival outcomes of children with the same diagnosis.

I truly hope you enjoy the visualizations as much as I enjoyed creating them.

— Stacy D. Brenes Coto
